About LYNX

LYNX Fiber Internet is a private internet company providing fast and reliable fiber network services to far-flung areas in and around Orion, Bataan. Founded in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic, we recognized the critical need for strong and stable internet connections throughout the locations often unreachable by traditional cell towers and major internet providers so we make it our mission to cater everyone who desires to purchase an internet connectivity even if they are living at the farthest reaches of Bataan.
