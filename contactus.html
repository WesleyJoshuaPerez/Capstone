FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I know if LYNX Fiber Internet is available in my area?

expand_more

LYNX Fiber Internet is currently offering internet services to residents residing in southern Bataan such as Orion, Limay, and Mariveles. If you are residing in Bataan but not on the aforementioned municipalities, it is better to submit a subscription plan application specifying your exact location so that the we could evaluate whether or not you can apply.

What are the requirements to successfully apply for a subscription plan?

expand_more

You must be at least 18 years of age, must submit a valid government ID (Philippine Passport, Driver's License, Unified Multi-Purpose ID [UMID], Postal ID, PhilHealth ID, or Voter's ID), and must submit a picture of your physical residency.

How much is the installation fee?

expand_more

Installation fees vary depending on your chosen subscription plan.

Where can I get support if I have internet issues?

expand_more

You can submit a maintenance request once you logged in on your account detailing your internet issues. The technicians from LYNX Fiber Internet would happily assist you to resolve any recurring issues you are having.

What are the available payment methods for paying bills on LYNX Fiber Internet?

expand_more

You can pay either by cash, GCash, or PayPal.

Does LYNX Fiber Internet provide any other services other than internet services?

expand_more

Unfortunately, we do not provide any type of services other than stable and fast internet services.

How long does it take for the approval of a submitted subscription plan application?

expand_more

It usually takes about 1 to 2 days.
